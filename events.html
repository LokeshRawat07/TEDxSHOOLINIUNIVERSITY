Past Events

Event 1

TEDxShooliniUniversity hosted its maiden flagship event on April 16, 2021. 'Metamorphosis' was the theme of the event. All speakers came from different backgrounds. Their work was different but the common thing in all of them was the metamorphosis these people underwent to realize and become the best version of themselves. They spoke about how they overcame the odds which held them back and how others can too. This forum, allowed them to share their stories and their ideas that were worth spreading.
