About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organised events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark a deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organised events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organised TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organised. (Subject to certain rules and regulations).

About TEDxShooliniUniversity

TEDxShooliniUniversity is a platform that hosts inclusive TEDx conferences at Shoolini University. Established in 2020, TEDxShooliniUniversity endeavours to unite the community of Shoolini University and Solan through the power of ideas, and spread them through the global TEDx forum. Our goal is to create an extraordinary forum of ideas to foster life-changing dialogues and to inspire action that brings real change in our community. We have hosted multiple grand events furthering TED’s vision of ‘ideas worth spreading’.
